FAQs

Who can use this website?

Current and future DLSU IS students can use this website, as it will help them plan out their schedule for the next semester. This is especially helpful for students who have failed their courses.


Can this help if I failed more than one subject?

Yes! This tool can accommodate multiple failed courses and will adjust/modify the generated schedule to the user's needs


Will this tool consider course offerings per term?

No. The tool will only recommend the courses based on the flowchart and will not base it on the MLS course offerings.


How does the scheduler decide what courses to prioritize?

The system prioritizes:

  • Failed or prerequisite courses
  • Courses required for future majors

Is this only for students who failed, or can shiftees use it too?

Students who failed or are planning to shift can use this!
